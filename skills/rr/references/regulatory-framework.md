# Applicable Regulatory Framework

When conducting an assessment, draw from these instruments as applicable. This list is not exhaustive; additional instruments may be relevant depending on the specific risk. **Search the web** if a risk engages a regulatory domain not covered below.

---

## Singapore — MAS Instruments

### Governance and Risk Management

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Risk Management Practices — Board and Senior Management** | Revised March 2013; under revision as at 2026 | Board and senior management accountability for risk culture, risk appetite, risk management framework, and independent assurance. Applies to all FIs including LFMCs. |
| **Guidelines on Risk Management Practices — Operational Risk (GORM)** | 2013; Updated consultation issued 06 March 2026 (closes 20 April 2026) | Operational risk management framework, business continuity, outsourcing. Proposed enhancements: disclosure obligations, change management, risk monitoring and reporting, oversight of branches/subsidiaries. Applies to LFMCs. |

### Technology and Cyber

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Risk Management Practices — Technology Risk (TRM)** | January 2021 | IT governance, cybersecurity, system availability, incident response, third-party IT risk. Not legally binding but actively enforced; MAS assesses compliance during inspections. |
| **Notice on Technology Risk Management (FSM-N05)** | Effective 10 May 2024 | **Legally binding.** Critical IT systems reliability, availability, recoverability, and customer information protection. |
| **Notice on Cyber Hygiene (FSM-N06)** | Effective 10 May 2024 | **Legally binding.** Administrative account security, security patching, baseline security standards, network security. |

### Business Continuity

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Business Continuity Management** | June 2022 | Service-centric approach to continuous delivery of critical business services. Requires: identification of critical business services, service recovery time objectives, dependency mapping, concentration risk assessment, BCM audit at least every three years. **Next audit cycle due by June 2027.** |

### Outsourcing

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Outsourcing (Financial Institutions other than Banks)** | Revised 11 December 2024 | Vendor due diligence (including employees), materiality assessments, outsourcing register maintenance, contractual arrangements, ongoing review. **FIs retain full ownership and accountability for outsourced functions.** |

### AML/CFT

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Notice on Prevention of Money Laundering and Countering the Financing of Terrorism (SFA04-N02)** | Updated 2024 | AML/CFT requirements for CMS licensees. Enterprise-wide risk assessment (EWRA) obligations. **Inclusion of proliferation financing (PF) in ML/TF risk assessments.** |

### Fund Management Specific

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Licensing, Registration and Conduct of Business for Fund Management Companies (SFA04-G05)** | Revised October 2019; FAQ updated September 2024 | Minimum licensing criteria, business conduct requirements, staffing requirements (directors, relevant professionals, representatives, CEO), compliance function requirements, risk management framework requirements. **LFMCs managing >S$1 billion require a dedicated full-time compliance officer.** |
| **Notice on Risk Based Capital Adequacy Requirements for Holders of Capital Markets Services Licences (SFA04-N13)** | Current | Financial resources must be not less than 120% of total risk requirement. **Breach requires MAS notification and remediation.** |
| **Notice on Reporting of Misconduct of Representatives (SFA04-N11)** | Current | Misconduct reporting requirements. |

### Fair Dealing and Conduct

| Instrument | Date | Applicability |
|------------|------|---------------|
| **Guidelines on Fair Dealing — Board and Senior Management Responsibilities** | Current | Fair dealing outcomes in product design, marketing, sales, advice, and complaint handling. |

## Verification Requirements

Before citing any regulatory instrument in an assessment:

1. **Verify current status:** Is it active, superseded, or under consultation?
2. **Verify correct title:** Use the full official title
3. **Verify relevance:** Is there a direct (not tangential) connection to the risk?
4. **Search if uncertain:** Use web search to confirm status and details
