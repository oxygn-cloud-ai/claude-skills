## Business Model

**Core Product:** Digital cash management and savings platform.

**Deposit Currencies:** SGD, USD, HKD

**Investment Strategy:** Funds are invested in a curated portfolio of conservative short-duration fixed income and money market funds:
- Amundi Funds US Short Term Bond
- Invesco USD Ultra-Short Term Debt Fund
- Taikang Kaitai Overseas Short Tenor Bond Fund
- abrdn Short Dated Enhanced Income

**Returns Display:** Daily in the app

**Withdrawal Terms:** At any time, no lock-in

**Payment Card:** Choc Card (generates interchange revenue)

**Critical Distinction:** The firm is **not a bank** and is **not covered by the Singapore Deposit Insurance Corporation (SDIC)**.

## Material Incident History

### Miles Programme Suspension (Early 2025)

In early 2025, the firm suspended a miles reward programme due to what management described as excessive exploitation ('gaming'). This triggered:
- Significant customer withdrawals
- Public criticism over communication handling

The firm subsequently stabilised and resumed growth.

**Risk Relevance:** This incident is a standing reference point for customer communication risk, product design risk, and reputational risk assessments.

## General Business Risk Dimensions

Apply these considerations to every assessment:

| Risk Dimension | Description |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Startup maturity risk** | Pre-profitability, rapid growth, small team relative to AUM and user base. Governance structures may lag operational complexity. |
| **Founder concentration risk** | Founder-led culture with high-profile CEO. Key-person dependency in strategic direction and external stakeholder relationships. |
| **Multi-jurisdictional scaling risk** | Each new jurisdiction (HK, UAE, JP) introduces distinct regulatory obligations, local staffing requirements, board composition requirements, and technology localisation demands. |
| **Customer communication risk** | Demonstrated by the miles programme incident. Direct-to-consumer model means operational or product changes have immediate, visible impact on a large retail user base. |
| **Custodian and counterparty dependency** | Depends on four major custodians and multiple fund managers. Concentration risk in any single custodian or counterparty is a standing consideration. |
| **Revenue model fragility** | Performance fee dependence means revenue correlates with market conditions. Card interchange and FX income are secondary. A sustained low-rate environment or market dislocation could compress revenue while operating costs scale with expansion. |
